Hello,

PyCon Nigerian 2018 is just around the corner, and just as we finalise preparations, we thought to give you these friendly reminders to help you better plan your attendance.

Firstly, if you have not gotten a ticket to the conference yet, now is a good time to obtain one from our portal at pycon.ng/tickets .

PyCon Nigeria 2018 will be a 3-day event, with the first day being only for tutorials (there is an amazing lineup of this). The venue for the event on the first day will be Vantage Hub, Ikeja - Lagos. Only those with a valid tutorial ticket will be allowed entry to the venue on this day. Please note that the tutorial ticket is quite different from a regular conference ticket.

The regular conference ticket, on the other hand, is valid for entry on the 2nd and 3rd days of the conference, where the venue will be at the LCCI, Ikeja - Lagos (more details below). So if you have purchased (or have been issued) one of the following ticket types, then you can rest assured it will be valid for entry to the conference venue on the last two days:

  • Student Ticket
  • Individual Ticket
  • Corporate Ticket

It is very important to be aware of the distinction between the different ticket types. Not sure what kind of ticket you possess? This should help you know: ...

Also note that holders of a Student-type ticket would be expected to back same with a valid Student ID.

Code of Conduct:

E very activity related to the conference including the conference after-party and the conference itself is bound by the PyCon Nigeria Code of Conduct.  All delegates/attendees, speakers, exhibitors, organisers and volunteers at any PyCon Nigeria event are required to abide by the   Code of Conduct . Organisers will enforce this code before and throughout the event.

Registration:

Please come along with a proof of ticket purchase either via a confirmation email or your ticket number.

Conference After-Party:

There will be a conference after-party on Saturday, after the conference, at Pub One, which is free for all to attend. Although the party is free to attend, note that you would be responsible for paying for your food or drinks. We have arranged with the restaurant to offer food and drinks at a discounted rate. You would have to show your conference badge for this. More details on this at the closing session.
